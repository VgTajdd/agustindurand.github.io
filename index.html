ABOUT ME


Results-oriented, highly-skilled and passionate senior software engineer with 10+ years of experience in software and game development. Strongly focused in problem solving and high quality code contribution. I like a lot C++, computer graphics and game engines. As a side project, I’m creating a game engine from scratch.

I studied in Mechatronics Engineering at UNI (Universidad Nacional de Ingeniería, Lima, Peru). As an undergraduate student, I started to write a lot of code for embedded systems, computer vision and artificial intelligence.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

I worked as a Game Developer at Bamtang Games for about 4 years. In that time, I participated in at least 10 successful projects.

Then, I worked as a Software Developer for about 4 years creating the software Cancha.

After this great time at Bamtang Games, I worked as a Technology Lead at Infosys. As a Tech Lead, I developed and tested mediation apps (backend in C++/Linux).

Then, I started as a Senior L2 C++ Developer (Software Designer) at Globant. In this position I worked in the Gaming Studio as a Senior Game Developer. The project was a very popular game (developed in UE4) and the client was Warner Bros Games.

After that, I worked as as a Senior Software Engineer (Software Architect) at BairesDev. Our client was Miso Robotics.

Finally, I returned to the world of games and I'm currently working as Lead Game Developer at Dead Monkey.

PROJECTS

I really enjoy solving interview problems so I also have a repository with a lot of them.
